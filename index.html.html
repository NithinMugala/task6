    Safety Mesurements

    Emirates and Etihad have been recognized for their exceptional safety standards. According to AirlineRatings.com, Emirates ranks third, while Etihad ranks fifth among the world's safest airlines. This recognition highlights the commitment of UAE airlines to maintaining high safety standards. All cabin crew, boarding agents and ground staff in direct contact with passengers will now don personal protective equipment (PPE) which includes a protective disposable gown over their uniforms, and a safety visor, in addition to masks and gloves.
